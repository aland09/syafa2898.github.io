Anama is using socio-nature approach to provide strong, relevant and advance analytical knowledge services.

Anama is using socio-nature approach to provide strong, relevant and advance analytical knowledge services. This approach mobilize transdisciplinary methods to identify and assess the mutually related aspect of social and environmental issues and problems.


We have scientifically rigorous techniques to capture co-evolving process of social change, livelihood dynamic and natural landscape transformation. This approach is pivotal for relevant stakeholders in designing development initiative of climate change mitigation and adaption and socio-environmental safeguard​​
