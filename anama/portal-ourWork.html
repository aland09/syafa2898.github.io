Our Fieldwork

We have carried out research for both private and public sectors with services including baseline research (social mapping), stakeholder mapping, risk management, HCV assessment, social impact assessment, and the implementation and assistance of donor-led projects.

Our working area has spanned all over the major islands of Indonesia with overall project duration from various partners and clients extending over 10.000 plus days.​
