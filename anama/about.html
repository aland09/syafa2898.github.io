WHO WE ARE

Anama Consulting is a consulting company based in Jakarta that offers innovative solutions in the implementation of sustainable, equitable, inclusive and collaborative socionature governance.​​

Our company's services are specifically focused on a socionature approach to address natural resource management problems and opportunities through the development of academically practical methodologies and social innovations, the production of the cutting-edge knowledge services relevant for business world, universities and the community. We also provide assistance and facilitation to implement solutions to socio-environmental problems for multi-sectoral parties​​

OUR VALUES

Adaptive

Developing resilience and innovation to face the ever growing challenges that requires us to develop novel and effective strategies to respond particular dynamic situations that we face today.​

Creative

Expanding horizon and developing ingenious approach in pursuing company goals through continuous learning and expertise development​​

Inclusive

Promoting diversity, openness, and partnership with different parties to strengthen our commitment and capacity to deliver services to our partner and client, while contributing for better socio-environmental conditions.​​

OUR MISSION

  • Mainstreaming holistic, integrated, and creative socio-environmental approach in solving natural resource management for the leading stakeholder.​
  • Developing effective, efficient, and practical methodology and social innovation that respond to existing socio-environmental problems.​
  • Delivering innovative and applied socio-environmental knowledge products valuable for business, academics, and society.​​​
  • Providing inclusive and collaborative assistance and facilitation to implement multi-sectoral solutions to socio-environmental problems​​.​
